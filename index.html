Words Worth Gavel Club

Words Worth Gavel Club is affiliated with Toastmasters International, a nonprofit educational organization that allows members to practice and improve their public speaking and leadership skills. Since 1924, Toastmasters International has helped people from diverse backgrounds become more confident speakers, communicators, and leaders.

About Our Club

Words Worth Gavel Club provides a supportive and positive learning experience in which members are empowered to develop communication and leadership skills, resulting in greater self-confidence and personal growth.

Through regular weekly meetings, members learn to practice public speaking, organize and lead meetings, and give constructive feedback. Our club welcomes youths from all backgrounds and experience levels.

Whether you're looking to overcome the fear of public speaking, improve your presentation skills, or simply become a more effective communicator, Words Worth Gavel Club offers the perfect platform for your growth.
